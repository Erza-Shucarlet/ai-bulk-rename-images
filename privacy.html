Privacy Policy

AI Bulk Rename Images & Files is a browser-based file renaming tool. All file operations are performed entirely within your browser using the File System Access API. Your files never leave your device.

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your files or filenames. The service runs entirely client-side — no file content, metadata, or rename operations are sent to any server.

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and collect anonymized usage data to serve relevant ads.
